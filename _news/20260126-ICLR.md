Our paper *[Local Entropy Search over Descent Sequences for Bayesian Optimization](https://openreview.net/forum?id=cPxmLZmFa7)* was accepted to **[ICLR 2026](https://iclr.cc/Conferences/2026)**. We introduce **Local Entropy Search**, a Bayesian optimization method for large, complex design spaces that decides what to evaluate next by reducing uncertainty about where an iterative optimizer (like gradient descent) will end up.
