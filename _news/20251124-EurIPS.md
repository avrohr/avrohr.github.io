Our paper [scipy.spatial.transform: Differentiable Framework-Agnostic 3D Transformations in Python](https://openreview.net/forum?id=eaN5y07hbw) has been accepted to the [Differentiable Systems and Scientific Machine Learning Workshop](https://differentiable-systems.github.io/workshop-eurips-2025/) at [EurIPS 2025](https://eurips.cc/) and selected for a **contributed talk** (6 of 59 accepted papers). The work was led by [Martin Schuck](https://amacati.github.io/), who will present it on 6 Dec 2025 in Copenhagen.
